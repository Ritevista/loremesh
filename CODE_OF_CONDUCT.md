# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

Use welcoming language, respect differing viewpoints, accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best for the community, and show empathy. Harassment, insults, sexualized attention, intimidation, doxxing, and other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour privately through the repository maintainers' GitHub profiles. Maintainers will protect reporters' privacy, investigate fairly, and may correct, warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban participants according to impact and recurrence. This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
